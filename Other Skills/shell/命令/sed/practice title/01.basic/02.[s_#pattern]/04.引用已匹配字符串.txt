title:
  已匹配字符串标记&
  
summary:
  &标记表示对匹配到的内容的引用。
  
example no.1:
- #BASH> echo 'love is love' | sed 's/love/& you/'

- love you is love

example no.2:
- #BASH> echo 'love is love' | sed 's/love/& you/g'

- love you is love you

example no.3:
- #BASH> echo 'love is love' | sed 's/love/& you/2'

- love is love you
